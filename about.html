About The Equine Depot LLC

The Equine Depot LLC is a family-owned and operated hay and feed supplier based in Griffin, Georgia. Born from a passion for quality horse care, we’ve built our business around sourcing the best forage products available — and delivering them with personal service you can trust.

We specialize in premium hay types that are often hard to find locally in the Southeast, including Alfalfa, Orchard/Timothy, Orchard/Brome, and Alfalfa Cubes. We also offer SweetPro nutritional supplements like EquiPride and EquiLix tubs — trusted by horse owners, ranchers, and livestock managers nationwide.

Whether you're caring for a single horse or managing a full barn, we treat every customer with the same level of respect and reliability. We don't just sell hay — we build relationships.
